What Is the Gentry Tifo?

A powerful, reusable display at Deepdale, the Gentry Tifo is a stand-wide tribute to honour all Preston North End supporters we've sadly lost. It’s built by fans, for fans, rooted in our proud Gentry tradition of dignity, respect, and unity. This is our way of saying: you may be gone, but you're never forgotten. Names & Recognition Every name on the Tifo was submitted with care by friends and family through PNE Online and social media.

We now have a finalised, respectful list — thoughtfully curated to avoid duplicates and ensure accuracy.
