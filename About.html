Who are we?


PNE Flags by PNE Online is a supporter‑run, fan‑funded initiative that creates tifos, surfers and banners to lift the atmosphere at Deepdale. From giant wavers to curated tribute pieces, everything we do is designed to bring colour, pride and noise to North End.

Our Aims

Atmosphere: Turn the Town End, Sir Tom Finney Stand and Invincibles Pavilion into a sea of blue and white.

Tribute: Honour our traditions — especially The Gentry — with respectful, permanent displays.

Community: Keep it fan‑led and transparent, with regular updates on PNE Online and socials.


Gentry Tifo

We’re currently fundraising for a stand‑wide Gentry Tifo — a unique tribute to Preston fans we’ve sadly lost. Donate via GoFundMe to help us reach the £10,000 target and bring this to life at Deepdale.

Latest News: We are looking for a partner/s to help us with a side project as part of this tribute. We wish to replace over 1000 waver flags which will be housed in the Sir Tom Finney Family stand, this will cost £2000 - if you would like to get some great publicity and support your local football club, get in touch.
