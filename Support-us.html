⚽ Where Your Money Goes

Every penny puts the Proud into Preston Cha

 Your donations fund materials, printing, artwork, safe storage, and matchday logistics. The more we raise, the bigger we go from giant surfers to full-stand tifos. All run by volunteers, all ground-approved.

🔍 Transparency & Trust

  • Regular updates on PNE Online and socials
  • Secure payments via Stripe
  • GoFundMe protects one-off appeals like the Gentry Tifo
  • No middlemen. Just fans in, flags out..

Quick Questions + How you can help.

Is this fan-run?

 Yes — 100% community-driven and fan-funded. Just fans, doing it for North End. Are displays club-approved?
Absolutely. Every large flag or tifo is fully approved and safety-checked by the Club. 

Can I stay anonymous?

 Yes.

Both Stripe and GoFundMe allow you to give anonymously if you prefer.

I can’t give much — does it still help?


Yes! 

A steady £2.50 a month makes a big difference over time. It helps us plan ahead and grow our displays.

🤝 It’s Not Just About Money

We’re a tiny team — but we’re trying to do big things. 

Out of 15,000 regular home fans, just a few volunteers are keeping PNE Flags going. If you love what we’re doing, your time could mean as much as your money. 

Here's how you could help:
 
Weekday Setup: Help prep displays, roll out materials, or repair damaged gear.

 Matchday Crew: Wave a surfer, hold a giant flag, or just grab one and get it flying
  
Anytime Support: Whether solo or with mates — you’re always welcome
